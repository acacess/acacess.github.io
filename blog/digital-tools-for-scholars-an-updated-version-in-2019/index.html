Digital Tools for Scholars (An Updated Version in 2019)

Published on May 12, 2019 by Acacess

Here, at Acacess, we understand the importance using digital tools in academic, so we spent some time cleaning and regrouping these digital services, hoping you can easily find the one you are looking for.

The original post was published by Thomas Crouzier in 2017 (see here). However, after several years, some of the service have become invalid, such as Citeulike; and some completely switched their product focus, for example, Colwiz became Wizdom.ai.

Also, our friend Lei Ma created Awesome Research Tools on GitHub, which collects some desktop tools for scholars. Maybe we shall merge these two lists one day.

Last but not least, we Acacess is on a mission to boost scholars’ productivity and we will release our tool very soon, so please stay tuned. To join our beta program, please send us an email to beta(2)acacess.com.
